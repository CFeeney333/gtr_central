Nissan GTR R35

The Nissan GTR R35 is the latest model in the legendary GT-R series. Launched in 2007, the R35 is a complete departure from its predecessors, featuring a new design, a more powerful engine, and advanced technology. The R35 has gained a reputation for its exceptional performance, making it one of the most formidable sports cars on the market.

Variants and Models

There have been several different models of the R35, including:

  • Standard GT-R: The base model features a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, all-wheel-drive system, and a sophisticated suspension setup.
  • GT-R Premium: The Premium trim adds various amenities, such as a Bose audio system, heated seats, and premium paint options.
  • GT-R Black Edition: This model features lightweight forged wheels, a unique black and red interior, and various exterior enhancements.
  • GT-R Track Edition: Aimed at track enthusiasts, the Track Edition features a stiffer suspension, lightweight wheels, and additional aerodynamic components.
  • GT-R NISMO: Developed by Nissan's performance division, Nismo, this model boasts increased power output, improved handling, and exclusive Nismo body components.
